ABOUT US

Since its founding in 1946, Yonex has been committed to “monozukuri (craftsmanship),“ innovation, and the development of sport, guided by our company philosophy “to craft a better future with unique innovations.”

TOURNAMENT

We run over twenty competitions a year with events suitable for intermediate to top county players.

BADMINTON CAMPS

Training camps are great for all players to develop during holidays. Most of are camps are for juniors but some are also available for competitive adult players.

INDIVIDUAL COACHING

Individual sessions can be arranged for yourself or a group of up to four players. Our experienced coaches can help you improve your weaknesses or give you a technique MOT.
